Manok na chicken with dark sauce!

charot adobong manok lang

Ingredients

  • Chicken with wings
  • soy sauce na black
  • vinegar na maasim
  • garlic pang aswang
  • bay leaves
  • peppaminta
  • oil
  • salt and sugar sa panlasa mo
  • LOVE

Oh di mo alam pano lutuin? oh eto

  1. sa bowl, marinate the manok with soy sauce, bawang, and peppaminta for atleast 29 minutes.
  2. painitin ang oil of your body in a pan and then saute the marinated tiktilaok.
  3. add the suka and dahon ng bay, wag i-stir, cover and let it simmer bruh.
  4. adjust taste with salt and sugar.
