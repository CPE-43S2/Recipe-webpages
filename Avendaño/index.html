Cream Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Unbaked 9-inch pie shell
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake pie shell for 12-15 minutes. Cool completely.
  2. Whisk sugar and flour in a saucepan, then whisk in milk until smooth.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until thickened and bubbly. Cook 1-2 more minutes.
  4. Whisk egg yolks, temper with some hot mixture, return to saucepan, whisking constantly.
  5. Cook 2 more minutes, stir in vanilla and butter.
  6. Pour into pie shell, cover with plastic wrap on surface, and chill at least 4 hours.
  7. Whip cream and powdered sugar to stiff peaks and spread on chilled pie.
  8. Slice and serve chilled.
