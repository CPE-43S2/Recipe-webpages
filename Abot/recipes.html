Carbonara

A creamy, comforting Italian pasta made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy, then add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. Beat eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
  4. Drain spaghetti and immediately toss with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, serve immediately.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 400 kcal | Protein: 18g | Carbs: 50g | Fat: 15g
