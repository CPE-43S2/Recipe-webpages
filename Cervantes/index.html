Simple Rice Cooking Steps Recipe by JLOC \ (•◡•) /

This is a simple recipe for a delicious rice dish by JLOC. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice or add more for more people.
  • 2 cups of water or first line of any finger.
  • 1 Pinch of salt to taste but optional.
  • 1 teaspoon of vinegar for avoiding spoiled.

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water.
  2. In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the rice and salt, then reduce the heat to low.
  4. Cover and simmer for 18-20 minutes until the rice is cooked.
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve with your favorite dish.
  6. Enjoy your meal! (｡♥‿♥｡)
